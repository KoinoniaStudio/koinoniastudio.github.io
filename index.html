Art From the heart of Wales

An expert in resin

Founded in 2021 Koinonia Studios is an art hub with a heart with all proceeds going towards support for young people who suffer from various mental health needs. Koinonia is a truly unique and empowering art studio. Every piece is hand made using expert techniques - as individual and unique as the young minds of Wales. Find out more about this incredible charity and browse the range currently ready to purchase. Better yet, why not pick up the phone and have a natter with Jo herself!
